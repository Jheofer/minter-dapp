Anonymity Industries: The Conglomerate

THE Physical Blockchain Comunity

@ Anonymity Industries we aim to bring the community together. Having a central hub to talk to someone seems to be an antiquated approach in this day and age, but by bringing the community together we have set our sights on saving the world! The face of Anonymity Industries will be a combination between an Internet Cafe and an event center. Hosting educational seminars, gaming parties, an even just coming in for some coffee- we will be THE hub for crypto. Powered by crypto, and renewable resources we will be empowering local businesses, and setting up outreach programs targeting drug abuse, hunger, and income disparity in our local communities. Our home base is in Albuquerque, New Mexico; but we plan on having a global presence by 2026.

- "Through unified strength of community, we will save this world!"

Road Map

Phase 0

A History

In January of 2021, I delved deep into the field of blockchain. I was fascinated by it, but not only that- I saw our financial future in it. Decentralized currency, controlled not by a select few but by all. I needed to live in this future world now!
I started planning away. After planning for a year and a half it is time to make this a reality!
Our entry point will be the NFT markets, but our project is so much more than that. We plan to bring the community together by having a local internet cafe and an event center, having a global presence by 2026; meaning global impact!
